Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Terra Rara is a house specialising in rare geological objects. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use this website or submit an enquiry.

Data controller: Terra Rara — contact@terrarara.com

What data we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The piece you are enquiring about
  • Any message you choose to include

We do not collect any other personal data. We do not use cookies. We do not use analytics tools. We do not track your navigation.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list, newsletter, or marketing communication without your explicit consent. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party, except as described below.

Third-party services

Our enquiry forms are processed by Formspree (Formspree, Inc., USA). When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers before being forwarded to us by email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. This may result in your IP address being transmitted to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data retention

Enquiry data is retained only for as long as necessary to process and respond to your request, and no longer than 12 months unless you enter into a transaction with us.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right of rectification — you may ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right of erasure — you may ask us to delete your data
  • Right to object — you may object to our processing of your data
  • Right to data portability — you may request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@terrarara.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the French data protection authority (CNIL) at cnil.fr.

Security

This website is served over HTTPS. Enquiry data is transmitted securely to Formspree. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact us at contact@terrarara.com.
