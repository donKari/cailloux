Terra Rara

The earth spent four billion years making these objects. We spent months finding them.

A geological fragment collected from a precise point on the planet is unique in the absolute sense of the word. No reproduction is possible. No equivalent exists. It will exist only once, in the hands of one person. Terra Rara's work begins long before the object reaches you — and it never involves a warehouse, a catalogue, or an algorithm.

Our commitment

A stone without
a story is just a rock.

Every piece we collect receives a numbered certificate of authenticity. GPS coordinates to the metre, exact time and date of collection, in-situ and studio photographs, and a full mineralogical description.

A QR code engraved on the display base links permanently to the piece's entry in our registry — accessible for life.

What Terra Rara is not
Not this
A minerals boutique

Terra Rara does not sell crystals, specimens, or collectibles by weight or category. Every piece is singular.

Not this
A wellness concept

These are geological objects. Their value is scientific, geographic, and aesthetic — not therapeutic.

Not this
A marketplace

Each piece is selected, documented, and certified directly by the house. There are no third-party sellers.

Not this
A museum shop

Our references are The Row, Aesop, Loro Piana — not the souvenir counter. One thing, done well, without announcing it.
